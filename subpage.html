Step 1 of 17

Step 1: Enter the Room

Enter through the door directly in front of the yeti. You'll see several boxes and floor buttons in this puzzle room.

Step 2: Move First Box

Push the box to the left of the floor button toward the right. This will begin positioning the boxes for the puzzle.

Step 3: Move Second Box

Push the second box in the right corner to the left (which will hit the block of ice). This creates space for the next moves.

Step 4: Position Box 2

Push Box 2 towards the floor button. You're getting closer to activating the first button.

Step 5: Align the Boxes

Push Box 2 towards Box 1 so that they are up against each other. They need to be aligned for the next step.

Step 6: Activate First Button

Push Box 2 onto the button. Success! The first floor button is now activated.

STOP HERE if you don't have the Ball and Chain weapon yet!

You must obtain the Ball and Chain weapon before continuing with Part 2 of this puzzle. The Ball and Chain is needed to break ice blocks.

Once you have the weapon, continue to the next step.

Step 7: Break Ice on Floor Button

Use the Ball and Chain to break the ice on the second floor button. Aim carefully and swing!

Step 8: Free Third Box

Break the ice on the third box in the bottom left hand corner using the Ball and Chain. Now you can move this box.

Step 9: Move Box 3

Push Box 3 toward the other boxes from Part 1. You're bringing all three boxes together now.

Step 10: Align Box 3

Push Box 3 across so it is up against both boxes. All three boxes should now be touching.

Step 11: Move Box 1

Push Box 1 straight across to the other side of the room. This creates a new configuration.

Step 12: Position Box 3

Push Box 3 to the left corner where it will be positioned by the doors.

Step 13: Reposition Box 1

Push Box 1 to where Box 3 was in the corner by the doors. You're swapping their positions.

Step 14: Move Box 1 to Starting Point

Push Box 1 to where Box 3 originally started. This continues the repositioning sequence.

Step 15: Push Box 3 Right

Push Box 3 to the right, moving it into position for the final moves.

Step 16: Align Box 3 with Box 2

Push Box 3 towards Box 2. They should now be touching and properly aligned.

Step 17: Final Move - Victory!

Push Box 2 towards Box 1. This is the final move!
